## Executive Summary 

### Problem Statement

One of the major confusion in everyday life of many households is *What should I cook today?* and this dilemma often leads to dishes being repeated too soon. Certain disliked dishes cooked repeatedly are often wasted or people lean towards ordering food *(junk usually)* which is also a major source for health related issues.

### Solution

This app aims to solve the problem by providing recommendations based on the past history of the user and other users. The app will do the following:

- Provide recommendation based on the user history.
- Provide recommendation based on other's history.
- Search when was a dish last cooked.
- Delete a dish from the logs.
